Overview

SDG 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all ages. This page helps you understand lifestyle risks and practical steps for better health.

Wellness Insights

  • Physical activity supports both body and mood.
  • A balanced diet reduces disease risk.
  • Regular sleep restores energy and focus.
  • Stress management protects long-term health.
These habits reduce the risk of obesity, diabetes, heart disease, and mental health challenges.

Healthy Habits

  • Exercise regularly 3–4 times a week.
  • Choose whole foods and stay hydrated.
  • Keep a consistent sleep schedule.
  • Take breaks to reduce stress and recharge.
Health is a long-term practice. Small daily choices build stronger bodies and minds.
