Daily Health Check

What is SDG 3?

SDG 3 is one of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. It focuses on ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all people.

Good health includes physical fitness, mental health, and access to medical care. A healthy population leads to a stronger and more productive society.

Common Health Problems Today

  • Lack of physical activity
  • Unhealthy diet
  • Insufficient sleep
  • High stress levels
These habits increase the risk of diseases such as obesity, diabetes, and heart problems.

The Importance of Exercise

Exercise helps maintain a strong body and improves mental health. It can reduce stress, improve sleep, and increase overall happiness.

  • Builds muscle and strength
  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Boosts confidence

How to Maintain a Healthy Lifestyle

  • Exercise regularly (3–4 times a week)
  • Eat a balanced diet
  • Sleep at least 7–8 hours
  • Stay hydrated
Health isn’t something you achieve once — it’s something you build every day.
