About SDG 3

Goal 3 of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals is focused on ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all at every age.

It includes reducing maternal and child mortality, ending epidemics, and improving access to quality healthcare.

Key Focus Areas

  • Universal access to primary health care and vaccines.
  • Improved nutrition and maternal health services.
  • Mental health support and substance abuse prevention.
  • Reduced risk of infectious diseases through prevention and education.
